#  <p align="center"> Welcome to Banger! </p>
</br>

## :handshake: About
>The App is A BANGER solution for Photographers and Instagram Influensers who's looking for the Best Photo Spots in the city thay are staying at.
>Banger is an open source and easy to use app, You can add cities and regions, add your spots and saved the one's you loved in your own favorites collection

## :sparkles: Features
>
> - Swift 5 based Application, designed and developed in Xcode 13
> - **Login** and **Sign-up** with any valid phone number
> - Chose/Change your **profile picture**
> - **Open new Region** and help the community grow
> - **Add and Share** your finds while travelling
> - **Save posts** that you wish to go to in a personal collection
> - Each post has the exact location **Marked on The Map**
> - **All Included App** the app has **links to the post's auther Instagram** and an Icon which will **direct to a navigation app (Apple's map)** already directed to the spot location
